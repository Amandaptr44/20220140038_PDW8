About Twice

TWICE (트와이스) is a South Korean girl group consisting of 9 members: Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. The group was formed through the survival show Sixteen and debuted on October 20, 2015 with the EP, “The Story Begins“, under JYP Entertainment.
